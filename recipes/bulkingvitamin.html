Banana Bulking Smoothie

Description

This amazingly creamy smooth is great for those who need or want to gain but also maintaining a good vitamins and healthy foods intake.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • 250ml of whole milk
  • 50gr of oatmeal
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon powder
  • 20gr of honey
  • Optional 30gr of peanut butter

Steps

  1. Just get all of the ingredients on the blender!
