Risoto Bianco

Description

This delicious italian dish never fali to amaze people for it simplicity and incredible combination of flavors.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of water
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oli
  • 2 cubes of vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of arborio rice
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped onions
  • 2 tablespoons of parmesian cheese
  • 1 cup of white wine
  • 3 tablespoons of butter

Steps

  1. Heat the water and dissolve the cubes of broth. Reserve it
  2. On a pan, low heat, put the olive oli and sauté the chopped onions
  3. Add the rice and sauté it untli it becomes translucid
  4. Add the wine and stir the rice untli the wine dry
  5. Add a ladle of the broth and salt
  6. Stir the rice frequently, adding another ladle untli it becomes al dente
  7. Turn off the heat, add the butter and the parmesian. Cover the pan
  8. Open it and stir the rice smoothly
